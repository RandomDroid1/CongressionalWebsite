S.O.A.T. BILL PASSED

  • Published 2006/11/8
  • Return to the News

The Safety of All Trees Bill, champtioned by Barbie Q, has recently cleared the House and Senate. This bill will ensure that no species of tree will be exterminated for no reason, such as the declaration that some trees are inherently superior to others. Now, we must ensure that the president does NOT veto this bill, as it is critical to the preservation of the environment.
