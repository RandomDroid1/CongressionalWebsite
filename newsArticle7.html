Rep. Barbie Q endorses new Transport bill

  • Published 2006/11/8
  • Return to the News

Representative Barbie Q. has officially endorsed the newest transport bill created by Joshua Treeshucker. This bill will give billions to the Department of Transportation (D.O.T.) in order to build an efficient and world-renowned public transportation system.
The bill allows the D.O.T. to establish a multitude of private contracts to effectively and efficiently complete this without going over-budget. The exact amount of time for the American people to start seeing the effects of this bill are unknown, but are likely in the 5-10 year range.
